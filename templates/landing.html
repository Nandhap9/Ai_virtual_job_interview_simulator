Common Interview Questions

Prepare answers for these frequently asked questions

psychology

Behavioral Questions

  • "Tell me about yourself"

    Keep it professional, 1-2 minutes max. Focus on education, relevant experience, and why you're interested.

  • "What's your greatest weakness?"

    Choose a real weakness you've improved. Show self-awareness and growth.

  • "Describe a challenge you faced"

    Use STAR method. Focus on your actions and the positive outcome.

code

Technical Questions

  • "Explain [technical concept]"

    Break it down simply. Use analogies if helpful. Show depth of understanding.

  • Whiteboard coding problem

    Think aloud. Ask clarifying questions. Consider edge cases.

  • "How would you design [system]?"

    Start broad, then drill down. Discuss tradeoffs. Show architectural thinking.
